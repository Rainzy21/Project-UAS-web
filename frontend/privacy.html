Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

What we collect

  • Account information (email, display name) via Supabase Auth
  • Saved movies, preference presets, and AI recommendation history
  • Usage data required to operate the service (rate limits, logs)

Subprocessors

  • Supabase — authentication and database hosting
  • Google (Gemini) — AI movie recommendations
  • DeepSeek — AI fallback for recommendations
  • TMDB — movie metadata and posters

Data retention

Recommendation logs are retained for up to 12 months, then eligible for deletion. You may delete your account at any time from your profile page.

Account deletion

Deleting your account removes your profile, saved movies, recommendation history, and presets from our application database. Provider backups may retain data for a limited period per Supabase policies.

Data export

Authenticated users can export their data via GET /api/users/me/export (JSON download from profile tools or API client).

Contact

For privacy requests, contact the site operator listed in the project README.
